String Transformation

LOWER(expression) - convert to lower-case
UPPER(expression) - convert to upper-case

LEFT(expression, number_of_characters)
RIGHT(expression, number_of_characters)

LTRIM(expression) - remove blanks from left (leading)
RTRIM(expression) - remove blanks from right (lagging)

TRIM([characters FROM] expression) - removes blanks or other specified characters (SQL Server 2017)

REPLACE(expression, searched_expression, replacement_expression)

SUBSTRING(expression, start, number_of_characters) - index starts at 1
